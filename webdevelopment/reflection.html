A peak inside my brain

Coming into college I had little to no experience in coding. All I knew is that I simply loved technology and all that it was capable of doing. The process of exploring the realm of programming was both stimulating and intimidating. However, as I got more involved in my classes, I saw that I made the best decision in terms of my major. My love for coding was fueled by the satisfaction of coming up with ways to achieve things and problem-solving.

While here, I have worked on a lot of assignments that have exposed me to a range of programming languages. Some tasks have been difficult and even tested my capabilities, others have been interesting and made me want to learn more. Despite the occasional difficulties and setbacks, I was and still am persistent because I know that each obstacle presents a chance for improvement. I have come a long way thanks to my commitment and I can honestly say I am beyond proud of myself.
